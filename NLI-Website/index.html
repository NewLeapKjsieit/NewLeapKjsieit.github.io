New Leap Initiative

New Leap Initiative is the Research & Development team of K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology. The team aims to promote space tech and Product Development skills among students. The team also has a registered Amateur Radio Club (viz. C. R.O.W.N) "Club for Radio Operations and Wireless Networks". The club aims to promote Amateur radio activities with the help of Amateur radio community among the students of various institutions.

Our Mentors

Dr. Suresh Ukarande

Principal KJSIEIT

Dr. S. K. Ukarande is an accomplished academician and a distinguished educational administrator. He is currently the Principal of K. J. Somaiya Institute of Engineering & Information Technology (KJSIEIT), Sion, Mumbai and the Associate Dean - Faculty of Science & Technology at University of Mumbai (UoM). In his 35 Years of exceptional career, he has enormously contributed at various . . . higher technical education leadership positions. He has successively led several accreditation initiatives at his work institutes. His wide experience as well as in-depth understanding of higher education and its correspondence to the industry needs has resulted into several awards and accolades to his institutes as well as rankings. Dr. Ukarande has visited 12 foreign Universities in 05 countries and has been invited as the Chief Guest for conferences and training programs. Dr. Ukarande considers education as the greatest convention for contributing to the society, and is at the help of academic affairs extensively.

Dr. Umesh Shinde

Dean of Research

Dr. Umesh Shinde is an assistant professor and Dean, of Research Development and Consultancy at K.J. Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology. He has been in the teaching field for more than 22 years. His professional goal is to contribute significantly toward the research breakthrough with scientific aptitude in faculty and students. He graduated in physics from Shivaji University in 1996 . . . and pursued a Master's in Solid State Physics from the same university in 1998. After Post Graduation, he completed a one-year diploma in Computer Programming. He completed his doctoral research in nanostructured magneto-resistive oxides . He is the faculty in-charge and mentor of the New Leap Initiative. His continuous efforts and support are truly the backbone of the initiative and his selfless contribution remains unmatched.

Mr. Vishram Bapat

Industry Mentor

Mr. Vishram Bapat is the Student Consultant at New Leap Initiative. He has mentored and guided various start-ups and student development programs and helped them grow along. His qualifications include: Bachelor of Electrical Engineering at IIT Bombay 1976, Member of Institution of Engineers, . . . Chartered Engineer, Technology expert on Panel of Ministry of Science and Technology, GOI, Delhi. Mr Bapat is one of the most experienced and resourceful people in the field of Engineering. He is the founding director at various IT Hardware and Software related companies. He has been Technology and Principal Consultant at leading International companies. He is a technical expert in domains such as Embedded Solutions, Wireless Sensor Networks (WSN), Application Software Development and Tactical Communication Networks. Furthermore he possess special expertise in Supply Chain Management, Airlines, Shipping, Defence and Homeland Security.
